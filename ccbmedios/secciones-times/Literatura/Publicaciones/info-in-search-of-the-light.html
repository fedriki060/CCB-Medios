In search of the light

Por: Juanita Bedoya Palacio 9B

Once upon a time, the Kingdom of Luminerea was known for the light and magic surrounding its borders, and it was said that inside its great walls, all kinds of magic and enchanted things lived freely; that was until an unknown evil, a strange shadow, threatened to darken the skies. To save Luminerea, a little girl warrior was sent to find an amulet that, according to the prophecies, would protect the magical land from any danger, restore the balance of nature, and return the stolen light. Her most powerful ability was her skills in sword fighting. No one knew what would be waiting for her in the way, but she was strongly prepared with her cristal shield and sword of light.

Along the way, all kinds of obstacles tried to stop her. Wild creatures found their death in brief combats, always ending with their rolling head. Wizardry puzzles found their ancient unknown answers in seconds; magical traps were easily fooled, all until she faced the entrance of an old cave, which could be thousands or millions of years old, and from the outside, she could see the blinding light of the amulet. Inside was the last test waiting for her, the final catch, a dragon. Ready to face the powerful beast, she raised her sword of light with determination burning in her eyes. With swift movements and her sword dancing in the air, she emerged victorious. With trembling hands, she grabbed the glowing amulet, feeling its magic course through her veins. With the fate of Luminerea resting in her hands, she knew that her journey was far from over, but she was ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead. And with the amulet at her side, she would bring light and magic back to her beloved kingdom.
